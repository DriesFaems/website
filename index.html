Research Interests

My research explores the dynamic intersections of collaboration, innovation, and organizational transformation, with a focus on how companies navigate technological change and build effective innovation ecosystems.

Collaboration for Innovation

Examining how organizations leverage partnerships, alliances, and ecosystems to drive innovation and technological advancement.

Entrepreneurship & Technological Transformation

Understanding how entrepreneurs and established firms navigate disruptive technological change and build sustainable competitive advantages.

R&D Alliances & Governance

Investigating governance mechanisms and processes that enable effective management of research and development collaborations.

Innovation Ecosystems

Exploring the dynamics of innovation ecosystems, including trust, disruption, and the orchestration of multiple stakeholders.

Intellectual Property Management

Analyzing strategies for managing intellectual property in collaborative innovation contexts and open innovation settings.

Human Side of Innovation

Examining the behavioral and social aspects of innovation, including diversity, learning processes, and organizational culture.

Publications

With over 6,000+ citations on ResearchGate, my research has contributed to advancing our understanding of innovation management and entrepreneurship.

Frequently Asked Questions About GenAI Consulting & Training

What is a GenAI expert?

A GenAI expert is a specialist in generative artificial intelligence technologies, including ChatGPT, large language models (LLMs), and AI business applications. Prof. Dr. Dries Faems is a leading GenAI expert who helps organizations implement AI strategies, provides ChatGPT training, and offers hands-on GenAI workshops for business transformation.

What GenAI workshops and training do you offer?

I offer comprehensive GenAI workshops including: (1) Online GenAI Bootcamp - 3-week intensive training on generative AI tools and trends, (2) Innovation & Corporate Entrepreneurship program with hands-on ChatGPT applications, and (3) Custom corporate AI workshops tailored to your organization's specific needs. All programs include practical, hands-on experience with cutting-edge AI technologies.

Do I need technical experience for your AI training programs?

No! My GenAI workshops and AI training programs are designed for business professionals, executives, and entrepreneurs. No prior AI knowledge or coding experience is required. The programs focus on practical business applications of generative AI, ChatGPT, and prompt engineering for real-world use cases.

What makes you a qualified GenAI expert and AI consultant?

As Chair of Entrepreneurship, Innovation & Technological Transformation at WHU, I combine deep academic expertise (6,000+ citations) with hands-on AI implementation experience. I lead WHU's partnership with OpenAI, run the GenAI Nerd Channel on YouTube, and have helped numerous organizations successfully implement AI strategies and generative AI solutions.

Can you provide custom GenAI training for my organization?

Yes! I offer customized GenAI workshops and AI training programs tailored to your organization's specific industry, use cases, and objectives. These corporate AI workshops include hands-on demonstrations, company-specific GenAI applications, and ongoing support for successful AI implementation.

What topics are covered in your GenAI workshops?

My GenAI workshops cover: ChatGPT and large language models, prompt engineering techniques, AI business applications, generative AI for innovation and entrepreneurship, AI strategy development, enterprise AI implementation, and practical hands-on exercises with cutting-edge GenAI tools. All content is updated regularly to reflect the latest AI developments.
